Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we provide free estimates for all our services. Contact us to schedule a consultation and receive a detailed quote for your project.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the local area and surrounding communities. Contact us to confirm if we service your specific location.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed and insured to provide all our services. We carry comprehensive liability insurance for your peace of mind.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity. Small projects may take a few days, while larger landscaping projects can take several weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

Do you offer maintenance services?

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance services for lawns, landscapes, and hardscaping features to keep your property looking its best year-round.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, checks, and major credit cards. Payment terms will be discussed during your estimate consultation.
